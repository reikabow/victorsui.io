Fake Random Points

The Fake Randomness Project began out of a math honors project and has expanded into several web apps and award-winning presentations. Its goal is to attempt to quantify the likelihood of some distribution being 'random'. I created a web app with Express and Highcharts to examine randomness in 1D and 2D distributions.
This project has been selected for presentation at the Illinois Undergraduate Research Symposium.
